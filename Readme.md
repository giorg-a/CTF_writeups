# 🎯 Cybersecurity & CTF Lab

Welcome to my central repository for Capture The Flag (CTF) challenges and security exercises. This space is used to document my hands-on learning and progress in offensive security.

---

## 📝 What's Inside

For every single challenge documented here, I provide a complete, end-to-end writeup including:

*   **Step-by-Step Walkthroughs:** Detailed, logical breakdown of the entire attack lifecycle from initial reconnaissance to final exploitation or flag capture.
*   **Screenshots & Proof of Concept (PoC):** Visual evidence and screenshots of critical steps, terminal outputs, and flag submissions.
*   **Full Solutions:** Clear explanations of the underlying vulnerabilities and the exact methods used to solve them.
*   **Custom Code:** Any custom scripts, exploits, or payloads developed to automate or complete the challenge.
